Quick and Easy Pizza

Description

Learn how to make pizza at home under a time crunch! This is the only recipe you'll ever need for fast, homemade pizza dough. It is so simple, anyone can do it! All you need is a quality homemade pizza dough, your favorite toppings, and an appetite. Let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 bag pizza dough
  • 16oz pizza sauce
  • 2 tsp dried Italian herbs
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp parmesan cheese
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 8 oz shredded mozzarella
  • ⅔ cup toppings
  • 2 tbsp basil

Steps

  1. Let the store bought door rise for 30 minutes
  2. Stretch dough out using a circular motion and transfer it to a sheet pan
  3. Brush dough with olive oil, add cheese and desired toppings
  4. Bake the pizza at 500 for 10 minutes
  5. Add basil during the last 5 minutes and let cool before slicing.

Great for a quick dinner on a busy night!
